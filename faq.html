FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything guests ask before booking Pine Grove Cabin.

How many guests does Pine Grove Cabin sleep?

Pine Grove Cabin sleeps up to 7 guests, in line with San Bernardino County STR Permit CESTRP-2021-00256. There are three bedrooms: a king primary suite, a queen room, and a bunk room.

Are dogs allowed?

Yes — up to 2 dogs over 1 year old, with a $75 pet fee. Cats aren't permitted due to guest allergies.

Is there a hot tub?

Yes, a private hot tub on the north side of the wraparound deck. There's a $100 hot tub fee.

Does the cabin have air conditioning?

No — Big Bear sits above 6,700 ft, and evenings stay cool even in summer. The cabin has central heating and ceiling fans instead of A/C.

Is smoking allowed?

No. Smoking inside the cabin is not permitted.

How many vehicles can we park?

Up to 3 vehicles, per the property's STR permit — plus the attached 2-car garage.

Is there a minimum age to book?

Yes, the primary renter must be 25 or older.

Is Pine Grove Cabin legally permitted as a short-term rental?

Yes. It operates under active San Bernardino County STR Permit CESTRP-2021-00256, which covers unincorporated Big Bear City.

What's the fireplace situation?

The living room has a gas-assist wood-burning fireplace, in addition to central heating throughout the cabin.

Is Pine Grove Cabin in Big Bear City or Big Bear Lake?

Big Bear City — the quieter, more residential east end of the valley, distinct from the Big Bear Lake Village tourist strip. See our Big Bear City guide for the full breakdown.

How do we book?

Reservations are handled through Airbnb, where you can see live availability, pricing, and message the host directly.
