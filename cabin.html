Good to Know

Practical details

No A/C — central heating & ceiling fans Exterior security cameras Self check-in via keypad Sleeps 7 3 vehicles, no street parking Up to 2 dogs, $75 fee

The cabin sits at 7,400 ft, so summer evenings and winter days both run cooler than you'd expect from Southern California. The cabin relies on central heating rather than A/C — pack layers. Parking is 2-car garage plus driveway (up to 3 vehicles total); street parking isn't available.
