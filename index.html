Made for family time

A Big Bear cabin that works for the whole family.

Pine Grove Cabin sleeps up to seven across a king primary suite, queen bedroom, and twin/full bunk room. The main living spaces keep everyone together; the downstairs Sasquatch Den gives kids a place of their own.

Location

Pine Grove Cabin is surrounded by tall pines and forest views near Bear Mountain Resort and the Alpine Zoo. Snow play, skiing, hiking, lake days, Village restaurants, and year-round mountain adventure are all within easy reach.
