Location

Big Bear City, not Big Bear Lake Village

The two get confused constantly, and it matters for your trip. Big Bear City sits on the quieter east end of the valley — closer to Snow Summit's back routes and the lake's north shore, with easier parking and a slower pace than the Village strip.
